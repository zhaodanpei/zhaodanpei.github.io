个人简介

赵丹培，博士，副教授，北京航空航天大学宇航学院航天信息工程系书记兼本科教学副主任。2006年毕业于中国科学院长春光学精密机械与物理研究所，获光学工程博士学位。2006年至2008年在北京航空航天大学宇航学院图像处理中心从事博士后研究工作，2008年被评为“优秀博士后”、“蓝天新秀”，出站后留校任教。2014年至2015年在美国罗格斯新泽西州立大学计算机学院做访问学者。担任北京图象图形学会青年委员会委员，担任中国图象图形学会遥感图像专业委员会委员，曾在《IEEE Transactions Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence》、《IEEE Transactions on Image Processing》、《IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition》等图像处理顶级会议和期刊、《IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing》等遥感领域顶级期刊发表论文50余篇，授权发明专利18项。同时担任多个遥感解译和模式识别领域期刊的审稿专家，包括《IEEE Transactions on Image Processing》、《IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing》、《Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters》、《Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing》、《Remote Sensing》、《Neurocomputing》、《Pattern Recognition letters》等。创建了 空天一体化智能遥感与视觉计算实验室（AIRVIC）。

先后获得国家自然科学基金、“十一五”、“十二五”总装预研项目、国家863项目、国家973子项目、空军预研、国家重点研发计划、航空基金、航天科学技术基金、航天支撑基金等多项项目支持，同时，也与航天四院、航天一院12所、航天五院总体部、东方红小卫星等航天院所和企业开展了多项关于遥感目标自动检测与识别方向的横向课题研究。其中与航天五院515所合作的横向课题，其成果已经应用到玉兔号月球巡视器中，实现了预期工程目标。与航天东方红卫星有限公司合作的横向课题，其研究成果成功应用到委2和巴遥等国外卫星中，在轨应用效果良好。

招生

AIRVIC实验室科研情况简介：赵丹培老师及其团队自2008年开始围绕复杂背景下典型目标探测与识别的关键技术开展应用及应用基础研究，其中涉及天基空间目标的信息处理、遥感图像处理与内容理解，弹载红外目标的检测与识别等应用领域，具体研究方向包括：
1、高分辨率遥感目标自动识别与解译技术；
2、视觉显著性理论及其在遥感领域的应用等。
3、天基空间目标信息处理技术；
4、高速嵌入式实时图像处理系统的开发与应用。

课题组常年招收博士后、全日制博士、硕士，请有意向同学将个人简历发至我的邮箱。联系我之前，至少简单浏览一下我们课题组主页近几年的论文，一方面方便了解我们在做什么，另一方面方便后续面试中有更好的表现。

Biography

She received the Ph.D. degree in optical engineering from the Changchun Institute of Optics, Fine Mechanics and Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in 2006. From 2006 to 2008, she was at Beihang University for postdoctoral research. From 2014 to 2015, she was working at the Department of Computer Science, Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, USA, as a Visiting Scholar. She is currently an Associate Professor and a Ph.D. Supervisor at the Department of Aerospace Information Engineering, Beihang University. Her research interests include saliency detection, target detection and recognition, image understanding, and their application in remote sensing images. She serves as a Standing Member for the Executive Council of Beijing Society of Image and Graphics. She founded and developed AIRVIC lab.

She received fundation from the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the “Eleventh Five-Year Plan” and “Twelfth Five-Year Plan” Pre-research Project, the National 863 Project, the National 973 sub-project, the Air Force Pre-research Project, the National Key Research and Development Project, etc. We have also developed a number of research projects on automatic object detection and recognition of remote sensing images cooperated with space institutes and enterprises. Our achievements have been successfully applied to the lunar rover Yutu, high-resolution earth observation and military application.

Recruitment

AIRVIC Laboratory Abstract: Danpei Zhao teacher began to explore key techniques of typical target detection and recognition in complex background since 2008 , which involves space-based space target of information processing, remote-sensing image processing and understanding, missile infrared target detection and recognition of application, etc. The specific research direction includes:

  1. Automatic object detection and recognition of high resolution remote-sensing image.
  2. Visual salience theory and its application in remote sensing.
  3. Space-based image information processing technology
  4. Development and application of high-speed embedded system for space-based real-time image processing.

Postdoc, full-time Ph.D. and master are accepted all year round. Please send the resume to my email address. Before contacting me, please at least briefly browse the papers of our lab in recent years. On the one hand, it is convenient to know what we are doing, on the other hand, it is convenient to have a better performance in the follow-up potential interview.
